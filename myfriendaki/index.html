Game Features

Environmental Puzzles

Make clever use of objects around you to solve puzzles and find secrets. Grab, throw, combine, and smash your way through the game in unique ways.

Immersive Horror

Delve into the dusty darkness of the mall, uncovering the history of those that were here before. Let gloomy lobbies and cramped corridors pull you along.

Dynamic Lighting

Discover the adaptive power system, bathing sections of the mall in light and dark. Combine generators, spotlights, and breaker boxes to light your path.
